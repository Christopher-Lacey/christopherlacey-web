Christopher Lacey

I created the EarthMusicGuide because I wanted to present a comprehensive and dynamic companion to the music of a strange beautiful planet. I wanted to share these ideas in a straightforward and simple way that would be easily understood, universal, engaging, and fun.

I became fascinated with music at a very early age and engaged with various musical instruments. I studied piano under Martha Boucher, guitar under Danny Strunk, and drums under Randy Everett. I played in the marching and jazz bands at Bartlesville High School and also formed "rock and roll" bands.

I then studied music at Trinity University in San Antonio under David Underwood and others.

During this period, I performed with an original band called The Suns of James.

Subsequently, I moved to the San Francisco Bay Area. There I formed an original, experimental venture that combined rock, jazz, and drum n bass with Shree Shyam Elton Bradman, Robin Eckman, and Andy Woods called Transmitter. I studied guitar during this period under Shan Kenner and Brian Stone.

I presently reside in Austin Texas and I am available for private and group lessons in person or online. I am also available for solo or ensemble performance. Samples of my music are presented here.
